[Cyber LLM Benchmark Hub](https://cyberllmbenchmarkhub.netlify.app/) is a centralized platform designed to track and compare the performance of frontier AI models on cybersecurity tasks. 

As the capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) in cybersecurity continue to evolve, evaluating their true effectiveness in specialized domains is critical. This project aggregates and organizes evaluation data across 10 distinct security domains, including:

* **Incident Response & Digital Forensics**
* **Vulnerability Analysis**
* **Penetration Testing**
* **Malware Analysis**
* **Threat Intelligence**

The hub features detailed benchmark data for complex evaluations like **SOCBench**, **ExploitBench**, and **SIR-Bench**, providing researchers and practitioners with a definitive source for understanding AI capabilities and limitations in Security Operations.
